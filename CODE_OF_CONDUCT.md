# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant code of conduct.

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment of any kind
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take
appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.
